Resort Management Software (With online booking)

This software project is an enhanced resort management system that consists of all the features and functions required for effectively managing a resort along with some new innovative features. The system allows users to check for room reservations, advance bookings, current bookings, free rooms, the status of the restaurant or party hall in the resort, and also the hotel staff payment status through SMS messages. It also notifies the system administrator about expiring staff contracts and payment dates in advance. The software system also sends room status notifications to the manager or concerned staff, such as when the room was last cleaned or when the last maintenance check was performed on it. This system can be used to easily manage large resorts because it notifies concerned individuals about all notifications on the desktop as well as SMS messages, allowing them to stay up to date no matter where they are.

Human Resource Management Software (HRMS) (Online/Offline)

Manually performing HR tasks is too complex and time-consuming. Leveraging the help of a technological solution like an HRMS can help you save time, cut costs, and manage your staff better.
Depending on your requirements, you could choose a simple HR Management Software with features that handle day-to-day administrative tasks such as employee management, leave management, and so on. Or you could choose a solution that addresses the strategic areas of human resource management like training and development, performance management, and recruitment.
